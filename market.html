Who is this for?

This service is designed for universities and their students. Universities buy the either the whole package or just the modules they want,

and that allows the students and staff to access the service. The staff side is designed with managing courses in mind, such as sending

out and correcting assignment, administering grades, and sending out notices. For students, it's designed to view and upload assignments,

take online tests, view module information, and access notes.
