Market and Customers

Universities procure OperO; staff and students use it daily—modular adoption, faster onboarding, and a single coherent experience across high-frequency campus operations.

Who is this for?

Buyers: universities and central IT—who need a governed platform path, predictable modules, and procurement that scales with maturity.

Users: academic and professional staff (course operations, grading, notices, module data) and students (submissions, assessments, materials)—without switching tools for every task.
