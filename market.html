Market and Customers

Universities choose OperO; students and staff use it every day—modular access, clearer operations, and a better experience across campus services.

Who is this for?

Institutions buy OperO: the full platform or only the modules they need. That gives IT and leadership control over scope and rollout while students and staff get a single, coherent surface for day-to-day work.

Staff use it for course operations—assignments, grading, notices, and module administration. Students use it to submit work, take assessments, follow modules, and access materials—without juggling a different tool for every task.
